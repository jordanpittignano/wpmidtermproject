A Look Into Our Services

At Fluff & Buff, we provide a range of grooming services tailored to meet your dog's unique needs. Each session prioritizes your pet's comfort, using high-quality products and techniques for a positive grooming experience.

BOOK NOW!

Bathing - $30

Treat your pup to a refreshing bath at Fluff & Buff! Our gentle, high-quality shampoos are tailored to your dog's coat type and skin sensitivity, ensuring a clean and comfortable experience. After the bath, we provide a soothing towel dry and a light brushing to leave your furry friend soft, shiny, and smelling delightful.

Dental Care - $20

Keep your dog's smile bright and healthy with our dental care services! We offer teeth brushing and oral health treatments designed to prevent plaque buildup and ensure fresh breath for your furry friend. Regular dental care not only promotes oral hygiene but also contributes to your dog's overall health and well-being.

Hair Cuts - Starting at $45

Our expert groomers specialize in stylish haircuts that suit your dog's breed and personality. Whether your pup needs a simple trim, a full grooming session, or a breed-specific cut, we ensure your furry friend leaves looking their absolute best. We take the time to discuss your preferences and any specific styling requests to achieve the perfect look.

Flea Care - $25

Say goodbye to pesky fleas with our comprehensive flea care services! We provide effective treatments and preventative options, including topical applications and flea baths, to keep your dog comfortable and free from pests all year round. Our team is also happy to offer tips on maintaining a flea-free environment at home to ensure your furry friend stays happy and healthy.
